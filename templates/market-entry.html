Study Destination Preferences of Nepalese Students
Nepalese students prioritize affordability and job prospects when choosing study destinations. They seek countries with better opportunities due to Nepal's competitive job market. Returning with an international degree and work experience enhances their careers. Key factors include immigration costs, work permit pathways, job opportunities, and stay-back policies.
